Methodology · the honest version This is self-reported diary data from 2,104 subscribers who opted into the Vesper 90-day study between Jan and Apr 2026. It is not a randomised controlled trial. We did not control for placebo, for sleep hygiene, for life circumstance, or for people who drop out (about 18% did; we excluded them, which biases upwards). Oura metrics are device-derived. Read this as a thousand anecdotes at scale, not as clinical evidence. We're running a proper double-blind RCT in Q3 FY27 and will publish whatever it finds.

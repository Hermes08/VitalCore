02Top Threats

Threat Types Targeting LATAM

Three primary attack vectors dominate the Latin American threat landscape.

Credential Theft & Account Takeover

Attackers use phishing, credential stuffing, and social engineering to compromise accounts across financial and government systems.

  • Phishing campaigns in Spanish/Portuguese
  • Credential stuffing from dark web dumps
  • SIM swapping targeting executives

Ransomware Attacks

LATAM organizations face aggressive ransomware campaigns. Costa Rica's 2022 national emergency proved no target is too large.

  • Double-extortion tactics
  • Supply chain compromise
  • Critical infrastructure targeting

Data Extortion & Breaches

Stolen data is weaponized for extortion, competitive espionage, and regulatory penalties in an evolving compliance landscape.

  • Customer PII exfiltration
  • Intellectual property theft
  • Regulatory exposure (Ley 81, LGPD)

Where we beat the market

On the metrics that change outcomes — active forms, disclosed sourcing, per-batch CoAs, zero blends — we are unambiguously ahead. A mechanically better product at the same or lower cost per active mg than our boutique rival.

  • Every active form is the bioavailable one
  • Every gram is sourced from a named supplier
  • Every batch is publicly tested, without NDAs
  • Free shipping and pause-any-time, globally

Where we tie

Ritual matches us on most formulation quality. If you want a women's-health-focused multi and don't need international shipping, it's an honest alternative — we won't pretend otherwise.

  • Both use the methyl forms of B₁₂ + folate
  • Both disclose every dose and exclude blends
  • Both certify the final-product manufacturer
  • Ritual has one clinical trial on the finished blend — we do not (yet)
02

Where we lose — honestly

Centrum, on price per milligram.

At $14/month, pharmacy-aisle multivitamins are five times cheaper per mg than we are. If cost is your dominant constraint and you don't care about absorbable forms or blends, Centrum works, and we'd rather you take something than nothing.

And AG1 wins on single-scoop convenience: if the act of remembering capsules is your biggest barrier, a powder you drink once wins every time over a bottle we optimize twelve ways.

— The Vesper team · we'd rather be honest than universal

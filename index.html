◉ The usual questions

Answers, honest ones.

When will I feel it?
Most people notice morning energy within ten to fourteen days, and sleep quality within the first week. Deep markers like vitamin D status take 60–90 days to move. We check in at day 30 and refund if nothing has changed.
Is one capsule really enough?
For the twelve molecules in Daily N° 01, yes — we use concentrated, bioavailable forms. If you have a specific deficiency, talk to a doctor and add a targeted capsule from the apothecary.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes, one tap from your account page. No calls, no retention scripts, no "are you sure". Skip a month, swap to a different formula, or change delivery any time.
What does "third-party tested" really mean?
Every batch is sent to Eurofins for contaminant testing (heavy metals, microbial, pesticides) and to a second independent lab for potency. The CoA for your specific batch is on the bottle — scan the QR.
Vegan? Allergens?
Daily N° 01 is vegan — D3 is lichen-sourced, omega-3 is from algae. HPMC shell. No gluten, soy, dairy, peanut. Made in a shared facility with tree nuts.
Why does it cost this much?
Bioavailable forms cost 4–8× more than the standard ones. We don't advertise on Meta, so margin goes to raw material, not ad spend. The twelve separate bottles would cost you $86.40.

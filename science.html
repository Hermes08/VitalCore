At VitalCore, there is a lack of craft in the way we build security products, and a lack of respect in the way we market and sell them. Many security teams have had enough

Results before tools

We focus on outcomes, not tool counts. Our recommendations are based on what actually reduces your risk — not on what generates the highest margin for us. We'll tell you when a free tool is the right choice.

Honest expertise

AI is not just a marketing term for us – it’s the reason we exist and the thing we’re best at. We aim to always be at the edge of what’s possible, pushing the boundaries of what AI can do for security teams.

Education over dependency

Everything we do starts with what’s best for our customers. In product, sales, marketing and beyond we start by thinking about what security teams actually want and need, not what’s best for us.

Rigorous, certified methodology

The best thing about security is that we’re all (pretty much) on the same team. We seek to continuously contribute to making that team successful.

Partnership, not a one-time engagement

Your security needs don’t end after the assessment report. We build long-term relationships: quarterly check-ins, ongoing monitoring options, and a direct line to our team when something feels off.

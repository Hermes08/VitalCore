How we choose

Three rules we won't break.

Rule 01
The geography matters
Terroir exists in supplements too. Norwegian omega-3 is different from Peruvian. KSM-66 ashwagandha has to come from central India. We buy the geography.
Rule 02
The form must be active
Methyl-B12, not cyanocobalamin. Bisglycinate, not oxide. The form on the label is the form your cells use. We pay two cents extra per bottle.
Rule 03
The supplier must prove it
Every ingredient ships with a Certificate of Analysis from the producer, plus a second third-party test in our own lab. No CoA, no buy.
